About Me

A bit about who I am

I'm a freelance web developer and video editor. I work with small businesses and creators who need honest, quality digital work done without the agency price tag.

My Story

I started because I saw a gap

A lot of small businesses needed websites and content, but agencies were either too expensive or too slow. Freelancers were hit-or-miss. I figured I could do it differently — show up, communicate well, and actually deliver what was agreed.

I've been doing this for a few years now. I've worked with restaurant owners, fitness coaches, event planners, and solo entrepreneurs. Most of them weren't looking for something flashy — they just wanted something that worked and looked professional.

That's what I focus on. No unnecessary complexity, no overpromising. Just clean websites, well-edited videos, and designs that serve a real purpose. If that's what you're after, we'll probably get along well.
