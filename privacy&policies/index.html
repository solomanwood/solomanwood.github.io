Plain language

This policy is written to be readable. If anything is unclear, just ask me directly.

The short version: I only collect the information you give me directly. I use it to do the work you've hired me for. I don't sell it, I don't spam you, and I keep it only as long as needed.

Section 01

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Solomon Wood ("I", "me") collects, uses, and handles personal information when you visit this website or engage my freelance services. I take privacy seriously and keep data handling simple and transparent.

As a sole trader, I am not a large data processor. The personal information I hold is limited to what's genuinely needed to communicate with you and deliver your project.

Section 02

What Information I Collect

Information you give me directly

When you contact me via the enquiry form, email, or WhatsApp, you may share your name, email address, phone number, and details about your project. I only receive what you choose to send.

Project materials

During a project you may share files, images, videos, written content, or business information. I treat all of this as confidential project material, not as personal data to be stored beyond the project lifecycle.

Website usage data

This website may collect basic, anonymised usage data through standard hosting analytics — such as page views and general geographic region. This data does not identify you personally and is used only to understand how the site is being used so I can improve it.

Section 03

How I Use Your Information

I use the information you share for three purposes only: to respond to your enquiry, to deliver and communicate about your project, and to send an invoice once the work is complete. I do not use your contact details for marketing, newsletters, or any form of outreach unless you've asked me to stay in touch.

I do not use automated decision-making or profiling of any kind.

Section 04

Who I Share It With

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. I do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

In the course of delivering a project, I may use standard tools such as Google Drive for file sharing, WhatsApp for communication, or email platforms for invoicing. These are standard business tools with their own privacy policies. I only share information through these tools where it is necessary to complete the work you've requested.

I may disclose information if required to do so by law or a valid legal request.

Section 05

Data Storage & Retention

Your contact details and project files are stored in standard business tools — primarily email, WhatsApp, and cloud storage. I do not operate a formal database of client records.

I retain project-related information for as long as is reasonably necessary — typically for the duration of the project plus a reasonable period afterwards in case of follow-up queries or disputes. Once that period has passed, I delete or anonymise the information where it is practical to do so.

If you would like your information deleted sooner, you can request this at any time by contacting me directly.

Section 06

Cookies

This website may use minimal cookies for basic functionality and anonymised analytics. I do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing scripts. If you prefer to browse without cookies, your browser settings allow you to disable or clear them at any time — this will not affect your ability to use the site or contact me.

Section 07

Your Rights

You have the right to ask what personal information I hold about you, to request a correction if anything is inaccurate, and to ask me to delete it. You can exercise any of these rights simply by sending me an email. I will respond within a reasonable timeframe — typically within 7 business days.

I will never make this process difficult. If you want your information removed, just ask and I will take care of it.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

My services are intended for adults and businesses. I do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has contacted me or shared information without appropriate consent, please let me know and I will delete it promptly.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

If I make any meaningful changes to this Privacy Policy, I will update this page with a revised date. For active clients, significant changes will be communicated directly. Continued use of this website or my services following an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Section 10

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how I handle your information, please reach out. I'm happy to explain anything in more detail.
